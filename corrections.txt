Main wind data sources used by Windy

Windy lets you switch between several forecast models. The wind patterns (including vectors) come from these:

1. European Centre for Medium-Range Weather Forecasts (ECMWF)
Default layer in Windy
High accuracy globally
Resolution: ~9 km
Updates: every 6 hours
👉 This is most likely what you’re seeing by default
2. Global Forecast System (GFS)
Global model from the U.S.
Resolution: ~13–22 km
Updates: every 6 hours
👉 Slightly less accurate than ECMWF but widely used
3. High Resolution Rapid Refresh (HRRR)
Very high resolution (~3 km)
Only covers the U.S.
Updates hourly
👉 Won’t work in Europe (important for your location in France)
4. Other regional models (depending on zoom/location)
ICON (Germany)
AROME (France, high resolution)
NAM (North America)

These can sometimes provide better local wind detail.
